Title


Breakfast 4 Servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)
  • Croutons, for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in diced carrots, celery, and red bell pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.
  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes (with their juices) and vegetable broth. Add dried basil, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Bring the soup to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Once the vegetables are cooked, remove the soup from heat. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, you can carefully transfer the soup in batches to a blender and blend until smooth.
  7. Taste the soup and adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Ladle the vegetarian tomato soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve with croutons if desired.
  9. Enjoy your comforting and flavorful Vegetarian Tomato Soup!

Tips:

For added creaminess, you can stir in a splash of coconut milk or cashew cream before serving. Customize the soup by adding other vegetables like spinach, zucchini, or mushrooms. Serve the soup with a side of crusty bread for a complete meal.
