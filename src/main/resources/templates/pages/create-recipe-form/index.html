Photo Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines when uploading a photo. Your recipe may be rejected if your photo does not meet the guidelines.

Do's

  • Landscape (horizontal) photos
  • Photos including your dish

Dont's

  • No portrait (vertical) photos or otherwise your photo will be displayed incorrectly
  • No people or pets in your photo
  • No personal information (name, age, etc.)
  • No content that violates our User Agreement
